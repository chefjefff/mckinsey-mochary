# Attribution

This repository packages publicly-available frameworks into a portable format for AI-assisted work. The MIT license (see `LICENSE`) applies to the packaging, synthesis, and integration choices in this repository. **The underlying intellectual frameworks belong to their original authors.**

## Source material

### `/problem-solving`

Distilled from:

> Charles Conn & Robert McLean, ***Bulletproof Problem Solving: The One Skill That Changes Everything*** (Wiley, 2019).

If this framework is useful to you, please buy the book. The 30 worked examples are exceptional and couldn't fit in a skill file.

### `/work-plan`

Step 4 of the Conn & McLean framework, extracted as a standalone skill because it's the most reached-for piece in day-to-day work. Same source as above.

### `/mochary-method`

Distilled from:

> Matt Mochary, ***The Great CEO Within*** + the open-source [Mochary Method Curriculum](https://mocharymethod.com/).

Matt open-sourced his entire method. If you want depth, go to the source.

## What's original in this repo

- The recognition that these three disciplines form a complete operating triad
- The packaging into portable skill files for Claude Code / Cursor / etc.
- The integration with AI agent workflows specifically
- The 3-question test as the operational gate
- The observation that AI agents need these disciplines *more* than human-only teams (because they can produce plausible-but-hollow outputs at scale)
- The before/after case study in `examples/`
